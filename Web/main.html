About Us

This website serves as a platform for users to help recycle different types of wastes they encounter in their lives. It is a user firendly, easy to use website where they can list their offers while providing necessary details for making contact. They can also view offers listed by other users which will contain all the relevant information about the client and the material. Users will also recieve rewards as they complete more and more transactions and earn points. It is a small initiative which aims to tackle the excessive waste problem by recycling it.
